Designing with Purpose & Clarity

Hi, I'm Laszlo. I'm a Product Designer based in Sopron with over 20 years of experience building digital products that simplify life. My approach is rooted in clarity, functionality, and emotional connection.

lightbulb Design Philosophy

I believe that great design is invisible. It should guide the user effortlessly through an experience without drawing attention to itself. My work focuses on removing friction and enhancing usability while maintaining a distinct visual identity.

When I'm not designing pixels, I'm likely exploring architecture photography or hiking. These hobbies inform my sense of structure, space, and natural flow, which I bring back into my digital work.
