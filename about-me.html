The Origin Story

From Nostalgia to Innovation

Everything started 20 years ago with my first iMac running Mac OS 8.6. The atmosphere of those early Risk-style games left a permanent mark on my visual language. Since then, I have spent two decades in the industry, designing complex systems for giants like Siemens and premium game studios like Greentube.

The Google AI Studio Build experiment was born from a desire for creative freedom. I wanted to see how far a designer could go with zero coding background using AI as a co-creator. It wasn't just about drawing; it was a deep learning process where I managed the project's soul, theme, and mechanics.

During the process, I faced real technical challenges: converting SVG vectors to Base64, optimizing data structures in assets.ts, and learning the hard way how to manage large-scale AI interactions to avoid crashes. This journey proved that today, a senior designer doesn't just create pixels—they design integrated systems.
