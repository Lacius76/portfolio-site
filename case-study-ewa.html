Trust Through
Dark Mode

I established a dark-mode fintech aesthetic enriched with premium gradients and Material Design 3 principles. The darker palette profoundly reduces eye strain during driving and inherently commands trust, a critical factor when handling user funds.

The digital wallet relies on intuitive swiping mechanics and clearly separated premium card designs. Cross-platform navigation was streamlined to ensure users are never more than a tap away from reserving parking or purchasing an e-Vignette.

The Designer as Builder Trend

From Mockups to Logic

terminal Deployable Android Setup

The 2026 'Designer as Builder' paradigm shifts focus from drawing static screens to building functional logic. Using Google Stitch and Next.js as starting points, I initially utilized Notebook LM to meticulously plan and gather all necessary documentation on Android environments.

This preparation took half a day, ensuring I had the capability to create my first-ever native app. From there, I utilized Google Antigravity and Flutter to transform those UI mockups into a fully functional, deployable Android APK.

"The handoff to engineering wasn't a static PDF, it was a working application with simulated payment logic."

By simulating complex payment logic and real-time state management, the build phase was completed in just 1 full day.
